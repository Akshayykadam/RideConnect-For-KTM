GATT Mutex Pipeline

Implements a strict, single-threaded Mutex BLE write queue. Re-orders navigation payloads and telemetry packets to guarantee sequential delivery, applying 3x exponential backoff retries when packet drops occur on the bike's receiver.

AES-CBC Cryptography

Bypasses manufacturer access limits via a reverse-engineered authentication suite. Executes a SHA-512 challenge key validation and maintains connection through a background daemon sending keepalive cryptographic packets every 1000ms.

GPX Engine & Tracking

Saves telemetry and geographic route indicators into clean GPX 1.1 records. Leverages high-fidelity haversine equations to log real-time speeds, altitude deltas, and slopes, exportable directly to Strava, Garmin, or Komoot.

RIDE DATA EXTRACTION ENGINE

By reading Android accessibility notification intents, RideConnect parses complex driving routes and updates directions within 10 milliseconds of turn notifications. It requires zero central web servers, ensuring your location data is strictly kept offline.
